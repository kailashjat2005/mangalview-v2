Dining

In House Dining

Experience the best in pure vegetarian cuisine at our hotel's in-house dining restaurant. Our expert chefs use only the freshest ingredients to prepare a wide range of vegetarian dishes, including Indian, Continental, and Chinese cuisine.

Start your day with our complimentary breakfast, featuring a variety of delicious options such as cereals, bread, fresh fruit, and hot beverages. Our breakfast menu is available for your perusal, and you can find a detailed list of our offerings in the attached PDF.

For lunch and dinner, our in-house dining restaurant offers a diverse menu that caters to all tastes and preferences. Whether you're in the mood for a spicy Indian curry, a hearty pasta dish, or a flavorful Chinese stir-fry, our restaurant has something for everyone. We also offer a range of snacks and appetizers to accompany your meal, as well as a selection of desserts to satisfy your sweet tooth.

Our restaurant features a comfortable and welcoming atmosphere, with plenty of seating to accommodate groups of all sizes. Whether you're dining alone or with a group, our attentive staff will ensure you have a memorable dining experience.

At our hotel, we believe that good food is an essential part of any great stay. Come dine with us and experience the best in vegetarian cuisine.

Rooftop Restaurant

Experience the ultimate in fine dining at our hotel's rooftop restaurant. Our restaurant offers a breathtaking 360-degree view of the city and the city palace, providing the perfect backdrop for an unforgettable dining experience.

Our expert chefs specialize in pure vegetarian cuisine, using only the freshest and highest quality ingredients to prepare a wide range of dishes. From traditional Indian delicacies to international favorites, our menu is sure to tantalize your taste buds and leave you wanting more. You can find a detailed list of our offerings in the attached PDF menu.

Enjoy the cool breeze and stunning views as you savor your meal, and let our attentive staff cater to your every need. Whether you're celebrating a special occasion or simply enjoying a night out, our rooftop restaurant is the perfect choice for a truly unforgettable dining experience.

Come dine with us and experience the ultimate in pure vegetarian cuisine, with a view that will take your breath away.
